# ChatBOT

### Step 1: Purchase and Register Your Cellphone Line

1. **Purchase a Cellphone Line:** Ensure you have a cellphone line that can receive SMS messages.
2. **Install on a Capable Phone:** Install this line onto a capable smartphone.
3. **Register Your Number:** Follow the steps outlined in this [**VERIFICATION LINK**](#) to register your number on our app. This will link your number with the WhatsApp Business Manager.

### Step 3: Set Up Your WhatsApp Business Profile

To fully take advantage of your WhatsApp Business profile, you need to personalize your business details. Follow the steps in this WhatsApp Business profile setup guide.

1. **Profile Picture:** Upload a professional profile picture.
2. **Business Details:** Fill out your business name, description, address, email, and website.

Completing these steps will help your business create a professional and trustworthy presence on WhatsApp.
